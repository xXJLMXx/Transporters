Transporters


We aspire to accomplish access to transportation through our easily accessible web app. By the end of the year we hope to have a wide range of vehicles, better web development, and meet the needs of those with different languages. The goals of this project are to create a web app that any person can use to acquire free transportation to places that are going to benefit and educate the users and public.
